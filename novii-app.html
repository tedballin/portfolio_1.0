Novii App

The Pitch

Novii is a comprehensive fitness solution that consists with a hardware component which can be installed on 90% of gym machines for tracking exercise activities, and a mobile platform for trainees to find certified trainers, for training with flexible schedule and in remote locations.

Type：

UI / UX Desgin
Self-initiated Project

Tools：

Adobe Ai, Adobe Xd

Interactive Prototype:

Link

Team:

Dmitrii Gusev (MSE student at SFU)
Leon Kokhnovych (Software engineer at Intel)

The Problem

After my user research and looking through fitness apps on market, a major yet not immediately obvious issue I found is that people can rarely achieve their fitness goals (strength training and bodybuilding) by using apps. Because such workout is physically demanding, it is hard for users to exercise correctly by reading text instructions and images. Most apps cannot keep track complex training activities without users manually type in information. The problems involved in both learning process and progress tracking.

Initial Design

My main responsibilities of this project are ideation, user research, user interface and experience design, and usability testing. The project initially started from the mechanical side which a hardware will be installed on gym machines and will keep tracking users’ training activities. After usability testing, I found that in addition to reviewing the training process, most of exercisers also expect to have professional guidance from certified trainers, but the training cost and fast-paced lifestyle are the major obstacles. I decided to integrate a trainer-trainee matching feature in the future in order to provide a more immersive experience and help users to achieve their goals.

Novii Redesign

Based on the feedback, as well as the pain points and design opportunities identified in the user journey, I redesigned the Novii App that can help the exerciser to find the right trainer based on the trainer's speciality, training rate, location, and ranking etc. The trainer and trainee can choose the training location and time that best suit their lifestyle and schedule.


With the hardware component installed on gym machines, it reinforces the trainee to complete the training tasks, and both the trainer and trainee can keep tracking the training progress (data collected by the hardware) on the mobile application. For this iteration, I sketched wireframes and created 30+ of interactive high-fidelity mockups using Adobe Illustrator and Experience Design.

Reflection

The team and I are very passionate about the project, as we believe the product could have real impact and enhance people’s training experience. Through this iteration process, I learned that how much it could transform and evolve from the original idea. As we are aiming to build Novii as a commercial product, the feedback from our peers, colleagues and friends are crucial for us to rethink its purpose and to integrate new features.

In terms of user interface design, there are still some problems and challenges. The trainer list currently has too much information, it looks crowded and the small buttons can be hard for users to tab. Creating training plans for clients requires a lot of typing from trainer which can be a daunting process. In the future, I would reduce the information shown in trainer’s list, and implement dropdowns and sliders for simplifying the process of creating training plans.
