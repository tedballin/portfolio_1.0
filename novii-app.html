Novii App

The Idea

Novii is a comprehensive fitness solution that consists a hardware component which can be installed on 90% of gym machines for tracking exercise activities, and a mobile platform for trainees to find certified trainers, train with flexible schedule and in remote locations.

Type：

UI / UX Desgin
Self-initiated Project

Tools：

Adobe Ai, Adobe Xd

Interactive Mockup:

Link

Team:

Dmitrii Gusev (MSE student at SFU)
Leon Kokhnovych (Software engineer at Intel)

Stretegy

My main responsibilities of this project are ideation, user research, user interface and experience design, and usability testing. The project first started from the mechanical side which a hardware will be installed on gym machines and will keep track users’ training activities. After user research (both qualitative and quantitative), I found that in addition to reviewing the training process, most exercisers prefer to have professional guidance from certified trainers, but the training cost and fast-paced lifestyle can be the obstacles in achieving their fitness goals. then we questioned ourselves: can we make a mobile app that works with the hardware, addresses these issues and provides a richer experience to the users?

Process

During the design process, I first created the user journey in order to lay out the activities involved in before, during, and after training, as well as pain points and design opportunities. Based on the findings, I designed the Novii App that can help the exerciser to find the right trainer based on the trainer's speciality, training rate, location, and ranking etc. The trainer and trainee can choose the training location and time that best suit their lifestyle and schedule.

With the hardware component installed on gym machines, it reinforces the trainee to complete the training tasks, and both the trainer and trainee can keep track the training progress (data collected by the hardware) on the mobile application. For this iteration, I sketched wireframes and created 30+ of interactive high-fidelity mockups using Adobe Illustrator and Experience Design.

Reflection

The team and I are very passionate about the project, as we believe the product could have real impact and enhance people’s training experience. Through this iteration process, I learned that how the original idea could transform and grow. As we are aiming to build Novii as a commercial product, there’re so many aspects that I have to consider and discuss with my team, the feedback from our peers, colleagues and friends are also crucial to us. For example, the original purpose of the app is to give user a visual representation of the training progres tracked by the hardware, then I questioned myself are these enough to make our product stands out in the market? After my user research and looking through other fitness apps, I found that people can rarely achieve their fitness goals by only using the apps. Because personal training is physical, getting professional guidance offline is necessary, then I decided to integrating the trainer-trainee matching feature into the app.

In terms of user interface design, there are still some problems and challenges. The trainer list currently has too much information, it looks crowded and the small buttons can be hard for users to tab. Creating training plans for clients requires a lot of typing from trainer which can be a daunting process. In the future, I would reduce the information shown in trainer’s list, and implement dropdowns and sliders for simplifying the process of creating training plans.
