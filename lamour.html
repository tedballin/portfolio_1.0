Lamour Weddings

The Project

This website was created for a wedding and event planning studio, and I completed this project with another design student from Emily Carr University. All we knew was the studio was looking to update their website, and we have two weeks to design and develop an website, and if we get selected, we would continue work on the website and get compensate.

Type：

Web Development Self-Initiated Project

Tools：

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery

GitHub Repo:

Link

Stretegy

We first looked into the existing website and concluded that the main purposes of the website is for displaying the weddings and events the studio has planned and designed in the past, and allowing the potential customers contact the studio in a direct manner. Considering the wedding and event planning has to be visually appealing, my partner and I decided that we need to use lots of visual elements such as videos and images to show off the work the studio has done.

Process

In terms of visual design, my partner hand drawn a new logo, and I selected the font and color of the website; I was also responsible for the web design and development: in order maximize viewers’ visual experience, I used side navigation which only shows when the cursor is hover on top. I created the carousel effect with JavaScript so that the viewers can see series of images of a wedding case or event and lead them to click and view the details. On the home page, the contact form allows the viewers to send their message, so the studio can respond to customers’ request through their prefered way of communication.

Reflection

Lamour Wedding was my very first freelance web design and development project, although our proposal did not get selected by the client, the experience was extremely valuable. In a short period of time, we researched and analyzed what are the important elements of this website, and how to utilize the website to lead to potential business. By working with a design student from different program at different school, I was able to adapt and learned how to work with people who have different knowledge background and mindset. I also learned some design tips from her during the process.
